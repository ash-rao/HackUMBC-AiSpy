About

In these trying times, we bring forth a nostalgic game - I spy. Simply start the game and our tool uses your webcam to find an object in view and sets you out with a clue to find it! We use object detection AI (supported by Google vision) to recognize objects in the image. It is recommended that you play in an area you are not completely familiar with for more challenging searches.

Privacy policy No captured images are stored on our system.
